In a roundabout sort of way, I've found that developing modules on these engines and gaming in general had prepared me for this field. Through both subjects I've grown this perspective on problem-solving; it's similar to tackling puzzles in games or brainstorming a maps layout and design. It's a persistence to try and get an outcome.

My journey as a developer has been marked by an unwavering commitment to continuous learning and strengthening my skills. Through dedicated efforts, I've had the privilege of creating and contributing to an extensive array of over a dozen projects.
